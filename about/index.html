About ekg.ninja

Our Mission


Our mission is to bring you accessible and engaging online medical education. We take you through every aspect of EKG interpretation in a systematic fashion so that when you encounter EKGs in the clinical setting you have a solid framework to work with. At the end of the day, we want to make you a better provider, and improve patient care.

Our Story


We are Emergency Medicine residents who felt there was a need for better core content education. Fed up with the current state of medical education, we set out to create something we wish existed. Our course embodies the educational needs of our generation. We have made short easy to digest and high-yield videos with quiz questions that enable you to learn what you need to know the fast and fun way.

- ekg.ninja team
